Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how we ("us" or "our") collects, uses, shares and protects information about you when you use the "dut" app ("App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information about you when you use the App. The app does not require any registration or login, and the data you input inside the app is only stored in your local device.

We collect anonymous usage data about the app, in order to improve the product experience for you. This data contains no personally identifiable information about you. Here’s what we collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics—stuff like what integrations you use, and the number of times you interact with the app.
  • Your country and OS language.
  • The version number of the app you’re using.
  • Crash logs.
  • The type of Mac you have and the version of macOS you’re running.
